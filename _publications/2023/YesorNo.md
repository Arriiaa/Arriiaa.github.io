abstract: >-
  "Yes or No" is a thought-provoking text adventure game exploring the depths of human duality, inspired by the theme of the 2022 Global Game Jam. In this intellectual journey, players face a series of choices that appear simple on the surface yet harbor profound moral implications. Each decision transcends the superficial dichotomy of "good" and "evil," delving deeper into the complexities of human nature. Through carefully crafted story branches, the game invites players to contemplate each choice, experience the weight of moral judgment, and confront the authentic echoes of their inner conscience.
