abstract: >-
  "City of Technology" is a strategic game blending ship collection, card development, and cooperative competition. Players step into the role of tech pioneers in a futuristic world, accumulating research points through gathering rare resources, developing breakthrough technologies, completing innovative projects, and constructing magnificent structures. Throughout the game, players can choose to collaborate with other pioneers on construction projects or negotiate resource ownership. The game concludes when all groundbreaking projects are completed and the city of the future rises. The player who accumulates the most research points emerges as the leading force driving human civilization forward.
